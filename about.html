About Terms of Service Abuse

  • Companies collect and sell the data of its users, while they remain unaware
  • Users click "I agree" without reading the Terms of Service in its entirety due to its verboseness
  • This then leads to them remaining unaware of how their data is used

Analysis of Consumers:

  • Studies show that consumers DO care about their data privacy.
  • A study conducted showed that users would charge 80 dollars for access to their personal information.
  • The same study also reported that consumers feel as though they should have control over who collects their data.

Feelings Aren't Enough

  • Despite consumers being against predatory data collection, no action has been taken.
  • Social Media companies collect as much user data as possible while privacy bills keep failing to pass.
  • The purpose of this website is to stand against the uneducation of consumers by breaking down exactly what they are agreeing to.
